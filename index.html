Entertainments

Arts:

Art is a creative activity that artists uses to express something. Art can be shown in many ways such as a simple painting or sculpting.




Music:

Music is an art form, and cultural activity, whose medium is sound. Music is performed with a vast range of instruments and vocal techniques ranging from singing to rapping.




Movies:

Movies are visual art-form used to simulate experiences that communicate ideas, stories, perception, etc by the means of recorded or programmed moving images. Movies can also be called motion picture or film.




Dining:

Dining is to eat dinner. People usually prefer eating what they like to eat. Some would dine without reservation at their own home and others would choose to dine at restaurants to take care of their business partners.
